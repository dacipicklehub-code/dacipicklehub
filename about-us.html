About Us

DACI Pickleball Hub

Located: Davao City, Philippines
📍 Google Maps

DACI Pickleball Hub is a dedicated pickleball space built for everyone—from beginners discovering the sport for the first time to experienced players looking for competitive and fun matches. Our goal is to grow the pickleball community in Davao by providing a welcoming environment where people can connect, play, and improve their skills.

We believe sports bring people together, and pickleball is one of the fastest-growing games that promotes fitness, friendship, and enjoyment for all ages. At DACI Pickleball Hub, we aim to create a place where players can unwind, stay active, and enjoy quality court time with friends, family, and fellow enthusiasts.

Whether you're coming for casual play, training sessions, or organized games, our facility is designed to give you a smooth and enjoyable experience every time you step on the court. We are continuously improving our services to make sure every visit is worth it.

Join us as we build a strong pickleball community here in Davao—one game at a time. 🏓✨
